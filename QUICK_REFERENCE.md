## ? FAQs

**Q: My barcode won't scan?**
A: Ensure input field is focused (tap it first)

**Q: Nothing happens when I press Enter?**
A: Check barcode is entered completely

**Q: How to close the modal?**
A: Click [Close] button or press Esc

**Q: Works on my phone?**
A: Yes! Works on all mobile devices

A: Yes! Keyboard entry works too
